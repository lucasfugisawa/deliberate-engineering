# Deliberate Planning

Invoke the `planning-strategy-selector` skill against $ARGUMENTS.

Classify the work by requirement clarity, risk, reversibility, and reach, then select and apply the matching planning lenses from the catalog. Scope to the real requirement first — decide what *not* to build — while keeping anything genuinely required for correctness. Ground the plan in the actual codebase, calibrate ceremony to risk, and decompose and sequence so every intermediate state is safe. If the work is trivial and safe, say so and keep it light. Capture every decision point with a recommendation and its rationale, and log anything you deliberately scoped out or skipped.
